REIGN APARTMENTS


Reign Apartments is a partnership business developed by the family of Mr.Byaruhanga. It is located in the heart of Kampala in Kololo Plot 13. It covers 50 acres and can accommodate 150 people. The apartment scenery is characterised by beautiful trees and flowers that provide a beautiful homestead and relaxing scenery for guests. The excellent and unique house designs make Reign Apartments a place worth living.
